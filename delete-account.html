Account Deletion Request

We respect your control over your personal data. If you wish to permanently delete your FutStack account and associated data, you may submit a deletion request at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request permanent deletion of your FutStack account, email our support team using the email address linked to your account.

futstackhq@gmail.com

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Email/login credentials
  • Prediction history
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • FutBingo activity
  • FutGuess activity
  • Associated gameplay records

Data Retention

Certain limited records may be retained where required for legal, fraud prevention, security, or compliance purposes.

Processing Time

Verified deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

Need Help?

If you have questions regarding your account or personal data, contact our support team anytime.
