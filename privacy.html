FutStack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

FutStack ("FutStack", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the FutStack mobile application and related services.

FutStack is an interactive football platform that allows users to participate in prediction challenges, multiplayer football games, leaderboards, and live football engagement experiences including FutPredict, FutBingo, and FutGuess.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use FutStack, we may collect:

  • Name, username, email address, or authentication credentials (if you create an account)
  • Profile information such as avatars or usernames
  • Gameplay activity including predictions, scores, leaderboard rankings, wins/losses, and in-app interactions
  • Multiplayer room participation data
  • Device information such as operating system, device type, app version, and crash diagnostics
  • Anonymous analytics data used to improve performance and user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide FutPredict prediction functionality
  • Operate FutBingo multiplayer sessions
  • Operate FutGuess matchmaking and gameplay
  • Maintain global leaderboards and rankings
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, cheating, or unauthorized access
  • Respond to customer support requests

Football Data Providers

FutStack may use third-party football data providers to deliver live fixtures, statistics, match schedules, team information, and related football content. These providers process data solely to support app functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party infrastructure providers including cloud hosting services, analytics tools, authentication services, and database providers to operate FutStack. These providers only process data necessary to provide their services.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not access your private photos or files
  • We do not track your precise location
  • We do not share personal data for third-party advertising purposes

Data Retention

We retain personal information and gameplay history only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain competitive integrity, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

FutStack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Users may also request account deletion at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
