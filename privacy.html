Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how FutStack collects, uses, protects, and manages your information across predictions, multiplayer games, and football experiences.

Last Updated: April 2026

FutStack ("FutStack", "we", "our", or "us") operates an interactive football platform featuring live predictions, multiplayer competition, and football intelligence games including FutPredict, FutBingo, and FutGuess.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email, username, or authentication credentials
  • Profile information such as avatars/usernames
  • Prediction history and leaderboard rankings
  • Multiplayer game activity and match participation
  • Device information and crash diagnostics
  • Anonymous analytics for improving app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • Operate FutPredict prediction systems
  • Run FutBingo multiplayer rooms
  • Support FutGuess matchmaking
  • Maintain rankings and competitive fairness
  • Improve app stability and security
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and cheating

Third-Party Services

FutStack may use cloud providers, authentication systems, analytics platforms, and football data providers to deliver fixtures, statistics, and gameplay features.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not access private photos/files
  • We do not access contacts
  • We do not track precise location
  • We do not share data for third-party advertising

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to operate FutStack, maintain rankings, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information, though no platform can guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

FutStack is not intended for users under 13 years old.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
